What is Knockout?
This will be a subtitle and will be an opening paragraph text
Built for dental students
ADAT Knockout is your instant access to reflective and complete online ADAT practice exams with the convenience of a click. Featuring ADAT formatted tests and time controls, users can best prepare themselves for the real deal under accurate and efficient conditions. Our program provides challenging questions that covers the level of breadth and depth of content, of all aspects of dentistry, tested by the ADAT. With thorough explanations for over 1000 questions, ADAT knockout provides the details and skills necessary to accelerate your success. With us, you’ll be in striking distance of an ADAT knockout.
Who made Knockout?
This will be a subtitle and will be an opening paragraph text
We wanted to solve a problem
Let me introduce my team; a group of dental specialists, general dentists and dental students with one common goal; to help our colleagues knockout the ADAT. Our exceptional team of OKU dental students have experienced the ADAT firsthand and have aimed to produce questions with the style, difficulty and content of the ADAT. With the collaboration of general dentists and various specialists, we’ve been able to recreate clinical case situations that stimulate the ADA’s new patient box questions as well. All in the strive to create the most reflective ADAT practice exams for you.
